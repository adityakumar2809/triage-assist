| Endocrine & Metabolic | Hormonal or metabolic concerns | Endocrinology | Tier 3 |
| Gastrointestinal | Digestive or stomach-related concerns | Gastroenterology | Tier 2 |
| Infectious & Febrile | Possible infection-related illness | Infectious Disease | Tier 3 |
| Liver & Hepatic | Liver-related concerns | Hepatology | Tier 3 |
| Musculoskeletal | Bone, joint, or muscle-related concerns | Orthopaedics | Tier 2 |
| Neurological | Nervous-system-related concerns | Neurology | Tier 4 |
| Respiratory | Breathing or chest-related concerns | Pulmonology | Tier 3 |
| Skin & Allergic | Skin or allergy-related concerns | Dermatology | Tier 1 |
| Urological | Urinary-system-related concerns | Urology | Tier 2 |
